A3: Visualizations

Here you will see my Assignment 3 visualizations.

Visualization 1: Global Sales by Genre and Platform

I chose to create a simple, colour bar graph because I would be able to represent the global sales of the different video game platforms sold.

Visualization 2: Sales Over Time by Platform and Genre

I chose to create a plot graph because I wanted to try something different and see the sales over time.

Visualization 3: Regional Sales vs. Platform

I chose another simple bar graph for this visualization because I wanted to simply see the number of sales from different regions for each video game platform.

Visualization 4: Tell Us a Visual Story

In this final visualization, I wanted to explore the sales in Japan over time because I was curious about this region due to the majority of the video games and platforms being from Japan.
